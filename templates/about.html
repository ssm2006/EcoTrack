About EcoTrack

A smart assistant that helps you understand, track, and reduce your personal carbon footprint through everyday actions.

How it works

  1. Log four simple daily inputs: transport, electricity, diet, and waste.
  2. The calculator converts your inputs to kg CO₂e using published emission factors.
  3. A rule-based engine identifies your biggest contributor or fastest-growing category.
  4. You receive 2–3 ranked, practical tips tailored to that category.
  5. Your history is saved per nickname so you can track progress over time.

Assumptions & Limitations

  • Emission factors are global averages and may vary by region and energy grid.
  • Diet emissions are estimated as a daily average per eating pattern, not per meal.
  • This tool is for awareness and habit-building, not precise carbon accounting.
  • No authentication — history is stored by nickname in local JSON files (demo only).
